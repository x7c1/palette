# Member

## Definition

A Member is a [Worker](../) that executes a single [Job](../../job/) under the oversight of a [Supervisor](../supervisor/). Members perform concrete work but do not make decisions about task coordination or job assignment — they focus solely on the Job they are given.

There are two kinds of Members:

- [Crafter](crafter/) — executes Work Jobs, producing deliverables such as code changes or plans.
- [Reviewer](reviewer/) — executes Review Jobs, inspecting a Crafter's deliverables and reporting findings.

## Examples

- A Member receives instructions and works within the scope of those instructions.
- A Member asks its Supervisor for clarification when something is unclear.

## Collocations

- execute (a Job)
- report (to a Supervisor)
